Drone Platform

CineLith
Cinema Drone

A compact, mid-frame cinema drone purpose-built to carry professional cameras such as the Sony A7S III. Engineered in an F330 form factor, powered entirely by LithCore and LithDrive — the first complete Aerolith aerial system.

Design Phase F330 Frame LithCore FC LithDrive ×4

The Complete System

Cinema-Grade Aerial
From the Ground Up.

CineLith is not a modified off-the-shelf drone — it is the final expression of the Aerolith engineering philosophy: every component designed, specified, and manufactured to work together as a single coherent system.

Airframe
F330 Format — 330mm Diagonal

The F330 format offers the ideal balance between payload capacity and portability for a cinema-class drone. The compact wheelbase allows for aggressive flight characteristics while still providing stable camera platform performance.

330mm diagonal Integrated mounting Vibration isolation
Payload
Sony A7S III & Equivalent

The camera mount system is designed around the Sony A7S III — a benchmark full-frame cinema camera. The payload bay accommodates camera and lens combinations up to the A7S class, with a vibration-dampened bottom plate and quick-release locking mechanism.

Full-frame camera Quick-release mount Vibration dampening
Avionics
LithCore + LithDrive Integration

CineLith runs entirely on Aerolith avionics. The LithCore flight controller occupies a central stack position, with four LithDrive ESCs integrated into the arm assemblies — minimizing wire runs and keeping the design clean.

LithCore FC LithDrive ×4 Integrated power bus
Flight Characteristics
Stability-First Tuning

PID gains on the LithCore platform are tuned for cinematic smoothness — prioritizing roll/pitch stability over agility to produce usable, stable footage from the mounted camera without a separate gimbal in many scenarios.

Cinematic PID profile Anti-vibration filters Hold modes
